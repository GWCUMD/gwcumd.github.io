Who We Are

At Girls Who Code, we believe that anyone can learn to code, regardless of their background or prior experience. We welcome everyone to join our community, regardless of their skill level, and provide a supportive environment where beginners can thrive and experienced coders can enhance their skills.

Mission Statement

Girls Who Code Believes in Promoting Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

At UMD, Girls Who Code strives to foster a welcoming environment for women in computing. We hope to contribute to closing the gender gap in technology and promote promote gender equality in computer science education. Our mission is to inspire high school and middle school girls as well as create a community for women in computing at UMD.
