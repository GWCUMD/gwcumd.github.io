Girls Who Code

The Girls Who Code chapter at UMD is committed to promoting gender equality within computer science education.

With each line of code, we strive to bridge the gap and create a more inclusive tech industry. At Girls Who Code, we believe that anyone can learn to code, regardless of their background or prior experience. We welcome all young women to join our community, regardless of their skill level, and provide a supportive environment where beginners can thrive and experienced coders can enhance their skills.
